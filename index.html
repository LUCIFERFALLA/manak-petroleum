Private Label Process

From specification to dispatch —
four steps, one accountable team.

A single point of contact takes your project from first conversation to a dispatched, labelled, and documented batch — typically within weeks, not quarters.

STEP 01 / CONSULT

Consult & Specify.

Our chemists work with your engineering team to define the right base stock, additive package, and performance specification for your end use.

STEP 02 / R&D

Formulate & Test.

Trial batches are formulated and validated in our ISO 17025:2017 accredited laboratory against IS, API and NLGI benchmarks.

STEP 03 / MFG

Manufacture.

Production runs at flexible quantities at our Harohalli facility — backed by 22,000 MT + KL of annual capacity.

STEP 04 / DISPATCH

Label & Dispatch.

Filled, labelled, palletized, and dispatched pan-India with batch documentation, COA, and consistent supply guarantees.
