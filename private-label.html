FAQ

Questions we
get every week.

Short answers. Full details on a call.

/ 01

What's the smallest run you'll accept?

Our pilot tier starts at 500 kg / 500 L. Below that we can run lab-scale samples for evaluation, but commercial supply begins at 500.

/ 02

Will you blend to my exact spec?

Yes. We work from your operating envelope (temperature, load, water, contact pressure) and develop the additive package in-house. Final formulation is signed off by you before commercial run.

/ 03

How is intellectual property handled?

Standard NDAs apply to every project. Formulation files belong to you. We do not cross-blend or share specifications with other clients — full segregation in our quality records.

/ 04

Can you label and pack to my artwork?

Yes. Pack formats from 500 ml through 200 L drums and bulk tanker. We coordinate label printing or accept your supplied labels — your call.

/ 05

Do you serve customers outside India?

Our primary footprint is India, but we have shipped to South Asia, the Middle East, and East Africa under buyer-arranged logistics. Talk to us about your route.

/ 06

What certifications do you ship under?

ISO 9001:2015 for manufacturing, ISO 17025:2017 for the R&D lab. Finished products are formulated to IS, API and NLGI specifications, with CoA on every batch.
