Industry 01

Automotive & CV.

India's commercial vehicle fleet is among the hardest-working in the world — long-haul payloads, monsoon mud, summer heat, and stop-start city duty cycles. Our engine oils and gear oils are formulated for exactly that.

We supply to fleet operators, workshop networks, two-wheeler distribution, tractor dealers, and CNG buses — through both proprietary brands (ZIGMA) and private-label arrangements.

Industry 02

Steel & Mining.

The hottest, dustiest, wettest, most punishing lubrication environment on earth. Integrated steel plants, iron-ore conveyors, crushers, kilns — they don't care about your viscosity index. They care whether your grease is on the bearing in six months.

Our complex thickener greases (calcium sulfonate, lithium complex) and open-gear lubricants are formulated for exactly this duty.

Industry 03

Construction.

Earth-movers, excavators, batching plants, dumpers, and pavers — under sun, rain, and constant load. Cold-start grease performance and hydraulic stability under thermal cycling are non-negotiable.

Our Calcium Base EP grease and shear-stable hydraulic fluids are specified across mid- and large-format construction fleets.

Industry 06

Marine.

Saltwater is the most aggressive lubrication environment short of a chemical plant. Deck winches, anchor windlasses, mooring fittings, and rudder bearings need a grease that holds its film, resists washout, and protects steel.

Our Calcium Sulfonate Complex (CSC) grease is specified across coastal and inland marine operations — fishing fleets, port equipment, dredgers, and naval auxiliaries.
