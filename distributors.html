Why Manak

A distributor partnership
built around predictability.

Most lubricant brands ask distributors to absorb supply shocks and pricing whiplash. We've built our partner program around the opposite premise.

01

Territory exclusivity

Committed partners get protected territories with clear boundaries — your customers, your relationships, your margin.

02

Predictable supply

Dual-plant production and multi-source base-oil contracts mean we don't run out. 12-month rolling commitments locked in.

03

Stable pricing

Quarterly rate cards. No mid-month surprises. We absorb short-term volatility so you can quote your customers with confidence.

04

Marketing & sales support

Trade-show kits, point-of-sale materials, technical training for your team, and joint field visits to your top accounts.

05

Technical backup

Direct access to our R&D chemists when a customer asks a hard question. Application notes, formulation help, troubleshooting.

06

A fifty-year-old name

Manak Petroleum has manufactured in India since 1973. ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 17025:2017. A name that opens conversations.

Requirements

What we're looking
for in a partner.

Honest about expectations from day one. If these aren't the right fit, we'd rather know — and so would you.

01

Existing trade footprint

An established presence in lubricants, automotive, industrial supplies, or hardware — and an active retailer / workshop / industrial-buyer base in your territory.

02

Warehouse & logistics

Minimum 800 sqft secure storage with bunding for chemical compliance. Ability to receive and re-distribute drum and pail formats.

03

Working capital

Indicative initial inventory commitment of ₹5–25 lakh depending on territory size and product mix. Open to discussion based on track record.

04

Sales bandwidth

At least one dedicated sales resource in the first six months. We provide product training and joint field visits during onboarding.

05

Compliance posture

GST registered, valid trade license, willingness to maintain stock records and participate in quarterly reviews. Standard distributor agreement.
