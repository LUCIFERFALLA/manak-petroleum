ZIGMA.
Your Trust, Our Responsibility — lubricants engineered for the Indian road.

ZIGMA is our flagship retail brand — a complete range of engine oils, gear oils, hydraulics and greases formulated for India's commercial fleets, two-wheelers, tractors and industrial machinery.

Built on premium base oils and modern additive technology, every ZIGMA SKU is manufactured to ISO 9001:2015 and tested before it leaves the floor. The MPC Economy Series delivers performance at a workshop-friendly price.

EMTEX.
Premium engine oils, gear oils and greases — the heavy-duty industrial line.

EMTEX is our premium line — heavy-duty diesel engine oils (TC Qwik, Chota Hathi, Xpresso), the Gear Protact gear-oil family, Drivtech motorcycle oils, AP Long Run greases, and Cooltech coolant.

Formulated to IS, API and NLGI specifications and trusted by commercial fleets, manufacturers, and earth-moving operators across India.
