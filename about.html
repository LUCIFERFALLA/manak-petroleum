Three generations.
One standard of quality.

Founded in 1973 and headquartered in India, Manak Petroleum has spent five decades formulating, testing, and manufacturing lubricating greases and engine oils for the industries that built this country.

Work with us Tour the facility
/ The origin

A family-run manufacturer that has never stopped specifying for the long haul.

In 1973, with a single blending kettle and a deep belief that India's industrial future would be lubricated by Indian formulators, the first batch of Manak grease left the floor.

Fifty years later, that kettle has become a fully integrated, ISO-certified manufacturing facility at Harohalli, Bengaluru. The team has grown to dozens of qualified chemists, plant operators, and field engineers. The clients have grown from a handful of regional workshops to multinational brands and billion-rupee distributors.

What hasn't changed is the standard. Every drum that leaves Harohalli carries the same accountability it did fifty years ago — Quality First. No exceptions.

Timeline

Fifty years,
told in milestones.

A condensed history of how a single-plant grease manufacturer grew into a pan-India lubricant company serving multinational brands.

1973
Founding
Manak Petroleum is established with a singular focus on lubricating greases for India's emerging industrial sector.
1985
Lithium & calcium-base expansion
Product range expands to include lithium-base and calcium-base EP greases for heavy-duty automotive and construction applications.
1998
Engine oils added
Manufacturing capability is extended to engine and gear oils, opening the company to the fast-growing commercial vehicle market.
2008
ISO 9001 certification
Quality management system formally certified to ISO 9001 — institutionalising the protocols that had been practiced for decades.
2014
Harohalli plant commissioned
The Harohalli manufacturing facility is commissioned in Bengaluru, consolidating production, filling, and warehousing on one integrated site.
2019
ISO 17025 accredited lab
In-house R&D laboratory accredited to ISO 17025:2017, enabling independent test results for private-label clients.
2024
22,000 MT + KL capacity
Combined annual capacity crosses 22,000 tonnes — among the largest independent lubricant manufacturers in the country.

Operating principles

What we defend,
batch after batch.

Internally we don't call them values. We call them commitments — because they are tested every single day on the manufacturing floor.

Quality First.

No exceptions. Every batch is tested to IS, API, and NLGI specifications and signed off before release. We don't ship what we wouldn't run in our own engines.

Customer Focus.

Formulations are driven by your application — not catalogue convenience. We listen first, run trials second, ship third.

HSE.

Health, Safety and Environment are non-negotiable. Effluent control, fire safety, and PPE compliance across the facility — verified, not assumed.

Innovation.

An accredited R&D lab and qualified chemists constantly improving formulations for evolving duty cycles — biodegradable bases, low-SAPS additives, EV-grade fluids.

Documentation.

Every batch ships with a Certificate of Analysis. Every formulation has a written specification. Every change is traceable, every quarter, every year.

Supply Reliability.

Multi-source base-oil contracts and dual-plant production protect our clients from supply shocks. Twelve-month rolling commitments. No ghost shipments.
